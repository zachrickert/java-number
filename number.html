The Rules of the Game

You only have a certian amount of guessing to guess the correct number. To make your guesses count you will be told if the number is higher or lower than the number that you guessed. After each succesful round you have a chance to unlock achievements. See if you can unlock them all.

  • Easy - 5 guesses to pick a number between 1 & 10
  • Medium - 8 guesses to pick a number between 1 & 100
  • Hard - 10 guesses to pick a number between 1 & 1,000
